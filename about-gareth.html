About Gareth

Meet Professor Gareth Wyn Jones

Dedicated to the exploration of energy's pivotal role in shaping societies, Professor Gareth Wyn Jones stands as an influential figure in the field of energy studies. As an Emeritus Professor of plant biology and bio-science at Bangor University, Gareth's expertise extends across the historical, environmental, and societal aspects of energy.

A quick Q&A with Gareth

Q: If you were just starting out on your energy journey, what would you want your younger self to know?
A: The fundamental importance of energy transformations, in their myriad forms, to all aspects of life; social, political and economic as well as biological.

Q: What would your advice be to someone who’s just starting out learning about energy?
A: Mug up some basic thermodynamics

Q: What classes have you liked teaching in the past?
A: a] Soil-plant relations (the continuity between geology soils microbes and plants): b] Basic biophysics and c] Sustainability
