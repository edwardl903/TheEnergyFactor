Why Use Less

Why Using Less Energy Matters

In a world where our every action leaves an imprint, the choice to use less energy is more than a personal decision – it's a global imperative. This section delves into the critical reasons why reducing our energy consumption is essential for the health of our planet, the stability of our economies, and the equity of our societies. We’ll take you through a brief journey of the history of energy and explain how it shapes our current and future world.


The Energy Journey

Embark on an enlightening journey through the annals of energy use and discover how it has shaped our world. From the primitive mastery of fire to the sophisticated energy networks of today, each step in this journey reveals how energy has been a pivotal driver of human progress and challenge. This interactive timeline invites you to explore the seven energy revolutions> that mark our relationship with energy – the innovations, the evolutions, and the shifts towards sustainability. The Energy Journey is not just a story of human ingenuity but a reflection of our collective responsibility towards a sustainable future.






Can Less Mean More?

From this timeline, you can see that climate change is not just about greenhouse gas emissions from fossil fuels. It is about the impacts and implications of our use of lots of energy, which creates more and more human demands on the Earth’s resources.
